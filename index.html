Hey, thanks for stopping by! I'm Rob, a full stack developer with experience developing products using both the LAMP and MEAN stack frameworks. I love solving problems and working out solutions for complex issues, so coding is naturally a passion of mine.

I currently reside in Sydney, Nova Scotia, Canada, and am a Student Success Coach with the UIT Startup Immersion Program. Working with UIT allows me to contribute to the local economy and workforce by providing prospective programmers with the mentors and resources that they need to learn the newest web and app technologies and be a force in the programming and development community.

When not writing code, I can be found cycling, playing baseball, or walking/running/hanging out with my dog and cat, Summer and Zelda.
